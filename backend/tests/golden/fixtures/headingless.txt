This plain text document has no headings at all. It simply describes, in a
few paragraphs, how the support team hands over unresolved tickets at the end
of a shift and what notes must accompany each unresolved case.

The second paragraph explains that every handover note lists the customer,
the current severity, the last action taken, and the next step the incoming
agent should perform, so nothing is lost between shifts.

The third paragraph reminds agents that entitlement questions and refund
boundaries are answered from the entitlement matrix, never from memory, and
that privacy escalations page the privacy officer directly.
